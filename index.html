About Me

I’m a 27-year-old writer and editor based in the San Francisco Bay Area. Currently I am pursuing my master’s in journalism at UC Berkeley after serving as a freelance staff writer at Black Enterprise Magazine and a copy editor at Latino media company mitú. Having over five years in the industry, most of my experience lies in editing and developing writers' skills to finalize captivating stories for publication.

Previously, I was copy editor for Black News Channel, The Pioneer Newspaper at California State University East Bay, and Occam’s Razor Literary Magazine also at CSU East Bay.

I received my bachelor’s in English with a focus in creative writing in 2016 from CSU East Bay.
